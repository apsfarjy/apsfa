Brought up:

  • Mahabubnagar, Nalgonda of Telangana region, Kurnool and Ananthapur of Rayalaseema Region, Vizag, Kakinada, Guntur and Machilipatnam of Andhra Region.

Academic back ground:

  • Post graduate degree in Agriculture, and P.G. Diploma in Forestry and allied subjects (equivalent to M.Sc., forestry) awarded by SFSC, Government of India

Career Brief:

  • On completion of Post graduation in Agriculture,(1983) joined the APAU Hyderabad and served the university till the end of December 1983. While serving the University, taught Soil Science and Agricultural chemistry to the students of Intermediate (Vocational course)

State service/ Forest service details:

  • Joined the state Forest service on 1.1.1984 in the cadre of Assistant Conservator of Forests and retired from service on superannuation on 30.06.2018. Thus in total rendered about three and half decades service in Forest Department and other than Forest Department.
  • Served all parts of erstwhile Andhra Pradesh State including 12 years in Telangana, 4 1/2 years in Rayalaseema, 10 years in Andhra, 6 years in Coastal Andhra regions.
  • Started the career in Adilabad district as Assistant Conservator of Forests in January 1986 and worked as ACF Normal, Sub-DFO, Chinnore till December 1989.
  • Later posted in Head Office where he served till September 1991.
  • Served various other organizations on deputation from September 1991 to December 1999 and had an opportunity to work as Executive Director, SC Corporation, Project Director, Housing, Project Director, DRDA/DWMA, Project Officer, ITDA, Sitampeta, Joint Director Social welfare/ BC Welfare, Joint Director (Planning), District Youth welfare officer, Adult Education officer etc.,
  • December 1999-March 2010: DFO, Medak, SS Hyderabad, DFO Mahabubnagar, DFO Hyderabad, DFO, Eluru, and DFO/CF, Kakinada
  • 06/2010 to 09/2012 worked as Conservator of Forests, Rajahmundry circle, Rajahmundry
  • 09/2012 to 04/2015- Regional Manager, APFDC Ltd., Rajahmundry region
  • During the period from Feb 2015 to April 2015, held the additional charge of Vizag Region also which deals with mainly Coffee Plantations.
  • April 2015 to August 2017- CCF, Kurnool circle, Kurnool
  • September 2017 to June 2018- CCF, Rajahmundry circle, Rajahmundry
  • Also held the additional charge of the CF WLM Circle, Eluru, WLM Circle, Tirupathi, Territorial Circle, Ananthapur and Territorial circle, Guntur, CCF SF Circle Ananthapuram, Vijayawada and Kadapa.
  • Held the charge of CEO EGREE, Kakinada and additional charge of the Director APFSA Rajamahendravaram.
  • Retired from service on superannuation on 30.06.2018.

Awards and Achievements:

  • Certificate of appreciations for the commendable service rendered as Project Officer, ITDA, Seethampet, Srikakulam District
  • Recipient of Certificate of appreciations thrice for the commendable service rendered as Executive Director, S.C. Corporation Srikakulam, Srikakulam District.
  • Received certificate of appreciation from the Hon'ble Chief Minister of A.P. on the occasion of Independence day celebrations on 15th August 1996 for the commendable service rendered as Nodal Officer in Srikakulam District in the implementation of Pajala vaddaku palana and Janmabhoomi concepts.
  • Received certificate of appreciation as District Manager, APSHC Ltd., Srikakulam Branch, Srikakulam.
  • As District Manager, APSHC Ltd., Srikakulam Branch, the district unit for the first time in its history was awarded FIRST RANK in the entire state in recognition of its overall performance for the year 1996-1997.
  • Under his aegis Mahabubnagar DIVISION was awarded "Indira Priayadarshini Vruksha Mitra" a prestigious national award.
  • He has received certificate appreciation for his outstanding contribution in reviving Kolleru Lake to its pristine glory.
  • He was awarded certificate of appreciation for the commendable service rendered in 2006 Godavari flood relief and rescue operations and received merit certificate for the meritorious service rendered in the capacity of Divisional Forest Officer, Eluru.

Academic bent of mind and research aptitude:

  • A research article titled "Trails on vegetative propagation in Poniki trees-Givotia rottleriformis" was presented in a National workshop held at Tirupathi during 1991 and the same was published.
  • During 2002-2004 conducted research on one of the important and endangered medicinal plants namely Decalepis hameltonii (Maredu geddalu) and standardized its regeneration techniques. A research article namely "Conservation and re-introduction of Decalepis hamiltonii-wight & Arn (Periplocaceae) in its Natural Habitat" was published in "Advances in Medicinal plants" authored by K.Janardhana Reddy, Bir Bahadur, B.Bhadraiah and MLN Rao.

Publications:

  • A booklet on "Community Forest Management - various Treatment Models" was authored by me and the same was published by the A.P. Forest Department and circulated among all the stake holders under community Forest Management in the entire state.
  • A Booklet on "Community Forest Management - Hand book for Community Forest workers" was authored by me and the same was published by the A.P. Forest Department and circulated among all the stake holders under community Forest Management in the entire state.

  • Russia (St. Peters Berg)
